W, A, S, D - Move
[SPACE] - Jump
[CTRL] - Sprint
[SHIFT] - Sneak
Left/Right Click - Inventory Action
1, 2, 3/Mouse Scroll - Inventory Select
[L] - Enter the Zone
[P] - Statsheet
[M] - Map

Objective: Retreive the 4 keys (1 in each location on the map)

Tips:
- Backstabbing enemies results in an instananeous kill.
